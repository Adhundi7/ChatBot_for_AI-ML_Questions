Typical approach is that as we move forward we will let the words be, if time is important, we will let the machine understand that time is important and extract the information from it, rather than we telling time is important. As we go towards powerful representations it will naturally happen. Then, this question will go away. Right now we are still thinking that we are the people who are to teach the computer. That’s the reason why we have a nagging feeling that isn’t time more important. The moment we are out of the picture this question doesn’t come up.
